# PawPal
PawPal - Online Pet Store  

PawPal is a mobile application designed to support pet owners in managing every aspect of 
their furry friends’ lives. From ordering pet essentials to tracking care routines and documenting 
notes, PawPal provides an intuitive, user-friendly platform that ensures pets are happy, healthy, 
and well cared for. Whether you’re a new pet parent or a seasoned animal lover, PawPal 
simplifies daily responsibilities and strengthens the bond between owners and their pets.
The app’s primary purpose is threefold: it helps users shop for pet essentials, track and manage 
their orders, and maintain personalized care notes for their pets. By centralizing these features 
into one seamless interface, PawPal makes pet parenting more organized, efficient, and 
enjoyable.
